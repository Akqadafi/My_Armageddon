Security groups act as identity-based firewalls, so allowing RDS access from an EC2 security group means only instances with that identity can connect, regardless of changing IPs, scaling, or instance replacement. This intent-based approach prevents internet exposure and lateral access from other instances, enforcing least privilege the way AWS designs internal service security.
